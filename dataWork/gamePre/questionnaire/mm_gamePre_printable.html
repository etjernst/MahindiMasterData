exp_explain
There are 20 beans in this cup. I would like you to choose some beans from the cup and place them in the intervals on the card. If you put ten beans in an interval, it means that in a given year, you think this is as likely to happen as to not happen.

Every bean represents one chance out of 20. If you put no beans in a given interval, it means you are sure this event will not happen. As you add more beans, it means you think the likelihood that this event happens is higher. If you put one or two beans, it means you think the event might happen, but it is not very likely -- one in every 5 or 10 years. If you put ten beans, it means that you think this yield is likely to happen in one out of every two years.

Again, there is no right or wrong answer. We just want to know what you think.
